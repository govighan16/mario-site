Meet Mario!

Mario, the iconic character of the Super Mario series, is a heroic plumber on a mission to rescue Princess Peach from the clutches of Bowser.

With his signature red cap, mustache, and overalls, Mario is recognized worldwide as a brave symbol.

He embarks on countless adventures, navigating through challenging levels and collecting power-ups like Super Mushrooms and Fire Flowers.

Meet Luigi!

Luigi, Mario's younger brother, is a green-clad hero known for his tall and slender stature.

Often seen as the underdog, Luigi has proven his courage on numerous occasions, helping Mario save the day.

With his Poltergust vacuum and excellent jumping abilities, Luigi is an essential part of the Super Mario team.

Meet Peach!

Princess Peach, the ruler of the Mushroom Kingdom, is a regal and gracious leader.

She is recognized for her iconic pink dress and her recurring role as the damsel in distress, often kidnapped by Bowser.

However, Peach is more than just a princess; she's a skilled athlete and a playable character in various sports and racing games.

Meet Daisy!

Princess Daisy, known for her vibrant orange and yellow attire, is a spirited and energetic royal of Sarasaland.

She's a fearless and competitive character who enjoys sports, racing, and adventures alongside Mario and Luigi.

With her confident and daring personality, Daisy adds a distinct flavor to the Super Mario series.

Meet Toad!

Toad, the loyal and courageous mushroom retainer, is a prominent character in the Super Mario series.

With his distinctive mushroom cap and friendly demeanor, Toad frequently aids Mario and his friends in their quests.

He's known for his speedy and resourceful nature, making him a valuable asset to the Mushroom Kingdom.

Meet Yoshi!

Yoshi, the lovable dinosaur, is an iconic character who comes in many colours.

With his unique abilities, including swallowing enemies and flutter jumping, Yoshi has been a valuable companion to Mario on many adventures.

His friendly and colorful appearance, along with his playful nature, has made him a beloved character among Mario fans.

Meet Waluigi!

Waluigi, often portrayed as an antagonist, is a rival to Mario and a mischievous character in the Super Mario universe.

His lanky appearance and signature purple attire make him stand out among the characters.

He is Luigi's cousin and has a similar appearance to him.

Meet Rosalina!

Rosalina is a serene and calm motherly figure in the Super Mario series, known for her association with the cosmos.

She is often seen with her Lumas, star-like creatures that accompany her on her celestial adventures.

Rosalina plays a pivotal role in games like Super Mario Galaxy, where she aids Mario in his spacefaring quests.

Power-ups

Super Mushroom

The Super Mushroom makes Mario grow larger, allowing him to smash through bricks and take an extra hit from enemies.

Fire Flower

The Fire Flower grants Mario the ability to shoot fireballs and burn enemies.

Ice Flower

The Ice Flower grants Mario the ability shoot iceballs and freeze enemies into solid blocks that can be used as platforms or to eliminate other threats.

1-Up Mushroom

The 1-Up Mushroom grants Mario an extra life, which can be a lifesaver when facing tough challenges.

Propeller Mushroom

The Propeller Mushroom grants Mario the ability to soar through the air. By spinning the propeller on his head, Mario can reach high places and access secret areas.

Tanooki Leaf

The Tanooki Leaf is a special power-up that transforms Mario into a Tanooki, a Japanese raccoon dog.

It grants the ability to glide gracefully through the air and turn into a statue, making him temporarily invulnerable to enemies.

Mega Mushroom

The Mega Mushroom is a super-sized power-up that turns Mario into a colossal giant, towering over enemies and obstacles.

Mega Mario can smash through blocks, pipes, and just about anything else in his path with immense power.

Super Bell

The Super Bell transforms Mario into a cat that grants him abilities of climbing walls, scratching enemies with his claws, and performing a pounce attack.

Boomerang Flower

The Boomerang Flower grants Mario the ability to throw boomerangs.

These boomerangs not only allow him to attack enemies from a distance but also return to him, making it possible to hit multiple foes in one throw.

Penguin Suit

The Penguin Suit transforms Mario into an adorable penguin. With it, he can slide on ice, swim gracefully in water, and shoot ice balls to freeze enemies.

Golden Flower

The Golden Flower is a rare power-up that grants Mario the ability to throw golden fireballs. These fireballs turn bricks into coins, allowing Mario to accumulate wealth as he progresses.

Super Star

The Super Star is a rare power-up that grants Mario temporary invincibility and a vibrant, flashing appearance.

During this state, he can defeat enemies on contact without taking damage, making it an invaluable tool for challenging levels and tough foes.

Enemies

Goomba

Goombas are common enemies in Super Mario. They resemble small brown mushrooms with two feet and no visible arms or legs.

Goombas are physically weak and are not much of a threat to Mario or Luigi, since a single stomp usually defeats them

Koopa Troopa

Koopa Troopas resemble turtles with green and red shells, some having wings. They are the minions of Bowser.

Koopa Troopas have a unique trait - they retreat into their shells when attacked. This can make them tricky to handle, requiring Mario to jump on them strategically.

Hammer Bro

Hammer Bros are notorious for their relentless hammer-throwing antics which deal deadly damage.

They continuously toss hammers from platforms or ledges, making it difficult for Mario to approach them. Precise timing and agility are essential to overcome these foes.

Piranha Plant

Piranha Plants are iconic, carnivorous foes that inhabit pipes throughout the Mushroom Kingdom.

These menacing plants emerge from their pipes when Mario approaches, snapping at anything in their path. Often they can spew fire balls out at anything near them.

Boo

Boos are mischievous ghosts haunting mansions and castles. They are known for their shy and timid nature, often covering their faces when approached.

When someone looks away they pursue with relentless determination, and it's said that the only way to stop them is to face them head-on.

Koopalings

Koopalings are a group of seven troublemaking Koopas, each with a distinct personality for causing chaos, led by Bowser Jr.

From the clever Ludwig von Koopa to the mischievous Lemmy, and the fiery Morton Jr, they are known for having various tough abilities to deal with.

Thwomp

Thwomps are massive stone blocks with menacing faces that hang in the air. These relentless Thwomps are known for their persistence and present a formidable obstacle for Mario.

When triggered, these heavyweights crash down with unrelenting force, attempting to flatten anything in their path. Only the quickest and most agile can get past them.

Bullet Bill

Bullet Bills are living projectiles that have become one of Mario's most recognizable foes. These high-speed, airborne menaces are missiles that won't stop until they hit their target.

They often have a warning sound before their entrance, provide opportunities for Mario to show off his incredible agility as he dodges and outmaneuvers them to continue his adventures.

Dry Bones

Dry Bones are skeletal Koopas that haunt abandoned hallways. Dry Bones are undeterred by ordinary stomps and often crumble into pieces when defeated but only to reassemble and continue pursuit.

Dry Bones provide an extra layer of challenge which requires creative ways to deal with them, whether through special power ups or precise platforming.

Bowser

Bowser, the ultimate final boss, is a giant, fire-breathing turtle wreaking havoc. He often captures Princess Peach and has an epic showdown with Mario.

Bowser is very intelligent and commands a legion of loyal minions, including Koopa Troopas, Goombas, and others. He can only be defeated after a long war with his army...
